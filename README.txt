Paddle.com Payment Gateway Plugin for WooCommerce

== Description ==

Paddle.com lets you sell your digital products through their payment gateway and handles all of your taxes automatically.
Your customers can pay using Paypal or Credit cards through Paddle.

**Thus you dont need to have Paypal account to receive payments from Paypal!**

Features:

*   Test the whole payment process in Sandbox mode.
*   Take payments right from your site in a popup.
*   Accept payments both from Paypal and Credit cards.

How to use:

*   Install this plugin first.
*   Make sure you have a Paddle.com account.
*   Make sure your website domain is approved under Paddle.com checkout settings -> Approved domains.
*   From your site dashboard, go to WooCommerce -> Settings -> Payments.
*   Click on Paddle.com payment gateway.
*   In the next page, enable the gateway, connect it with your Paddle account and finally save.
*   Now you should see Paddle.com gateway is working in your checkout page.

Plugin inspired & started from [this repo](https://github.com/hasinhayder/paddle-woocommerce-3)
Active development and contribute to [this repo](https://github.com/MehbubRashid/wc-paddle-payment-gateway)
